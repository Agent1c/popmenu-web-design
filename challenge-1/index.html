SEAFOOD

Seafood lovers know that the magic of the ocean doesn’t just lie in the taste of a delicious meal—it’s also in the experience of enjoying something fresh, flavorful, and full of character. Whether you’re savoring a plate of oysters, indulging in a hearty seafood boil, or enjoying sushi at your favorite spot, there’s something about seafood that feels luxurious and fun.

Our Menu

Seafood Plate

Our seafood plate is a delicious combination of shrimp, crab, and lobster. Served with a side of garlic butter, this dish is perfect for sharing with friends or enjoying on your own.

Fresh Oysters

Our fresh oysters are shucked to order and served with a side of cocktail sauce and lemon wedges. Enjoy them on the half shell or try them grilled with garlic butter.

Seafood Boil

Our seafood boil is a feast for the senses. Enjoy a combination of shrimp, crab, lobster, andouille sausage, corn, and potatoes, all boiled in a flavorful broth and served with a side of garlic butter.
