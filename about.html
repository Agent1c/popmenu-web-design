Our Story

Seafood Boiler is a fast-casual seafood restaurant that specializes in serving fresh, flavorful seafood dishes. Our menu features a variety of seafood options, including shrimp, crab, lobster, and oysters. We take pride in sourcing our seafood from local suppliers and preparing it in-house to ensure that every dish is fresh and delicious. Whether you’re in the mood for a seafood boil, a plate of oysters, or a sushi roll, we have something for everyone to enjoy.

Our Mission

At Seafood Boiler, our mission is to provide our customers with a memorable dining experience that celebrates the flavors of the ocean. We are committed to serving fresh, high-quality seafood dishes that are prepared with care and attention to detail. Our team is dedicated to providing excellent customer service and creating a welcoming atmosphere for all of our guests. We invite you to join us for a meal and experience the magic of seafood for yourself.

Our Team

Our team at Seafood Boiler is made up of passionate individuals who share a love for seafood and a commitment to excellence. From our chefs to our servers, everyone on our team plays a vital role in creating a positive dining experience for our guests. We take pride in working together to deliver exceptional service and delicious food to our customers. We look forward to welcoming you to our restaurant and sharing our love of seafood with you.
